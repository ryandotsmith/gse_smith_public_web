Smith Transportation is a full service, over-the-road refrigerated transportation provider dedicated to providing the best service and reliability in the industry. Smith Transportation was built on the principle that our reputation is our most important asset. We believe in providing the BEST service to our customers on every single load. We maintain our reputation by doing the things that other carriers do not:

  • We hire only the very best operators.  Each driver is qualified to drive for Smith only after clearing a rigorous process.  We ensure that each our driver shares the same level of commitment to the customer as we do.
  • We follow through on what we promise.  We do not overbook freight when the equipment is not available.  At the same time, we highly value our customers and understand the importance of committing equipment year-round, even during Holidays and busy seasons.
  • We treat each and every load with care. Our priority is on-time deliveries.  Whether the load calls for team service or re-powering of the equipment—we utilize all resources to ensure on-time deliveries.

Customers who work with Smith Transportation are able to their attention on growing their business, not managing the minute details of the transportation business. Whether you have special handling needs, an expedited shipment, or simply want very best service on each and every load, Smith Transportation is your carrier of choice.
