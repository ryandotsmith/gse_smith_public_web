Smith Transportation knows the importance of each customer and each load. As a refrigerated carrier with a proven track record, Smith Transportation delivers by offering a unique range of services:

  • Custom Care On Every Load: Smith Transportation understands that each load is important and has its own unique set of demands.   Each Smith driver is trained to systemically monitor product temperatures and to notify dispatch of any variables in product temperature or condition.  Additionally, Smith drivers are trained to close monitor loading procedures and product counts to minimize the risk of overages, shortages or damage to product. 
  • On time Deliveries: Smith Transportation knows the importance of on-time deliveries.  We recognize that perishable goods—and many non-perishable goods, have a short shelf life and just in time delivery is essential.  When problems arise, Smith is quick to offer a viable solution to ensure the product is delivered on-time, every time.
  • Dependable Equipment: Each Smith trailer is late-model reefer that is clean and free of defects.  Your freight will ride comfortably in its ideal temperature throughout the length of haul.   All Smith reefers are routinely maintained and calibrated to ensure the proper cooling is taking place. 
  • Elite Driver Pool: Smith Transportation recognizes that not all carriers are share the same level of service.   Each carrier that is contracted with Smith Transportation must meet our stringent standards, including qualifying safety scores, minimum insurance coverage with reputable providers, a proven track record with verifiable experience, and 24/7 availability.
