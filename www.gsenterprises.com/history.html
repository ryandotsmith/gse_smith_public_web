GS Enterprises was founded in January of 1999. Greg Smith started the company in the basement of his home in Lenexa, KS. On his first day in business, Mr. Smith personally instilled the lasting mark of the company: going the extra mile for the customer to provide uncommon service.

Whether handling fresh produce, frozen loads or dry goods, Mr. Smith ensures that each load will be handled with care and attention. By personally talking to and dispatching each driver, Mr. Smith proved to be a reliable provider for customers. The word quickly spread and the business soon outgrew the basement.

GS Enterprises is now co-located with Smith Transportation, the asset-based refrigerated carrier, in Kansas City, KS. Mr. Smith's vision of a company that provides exceptional customer service has only expanded. Today, GS Enterprises works with over 3,000 quality carriers for over 5,000 shippers all over the country.
